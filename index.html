DAYAL PUBLIC SCHOOL

Dayal Public School is a place of teaching, where your children can feel comfortable while learning. As a parent, what more can you ask for?

Our Aim:

EDUCATION

Education is not only about the past and present, but it is also the key to the future. It will help discipline our children for the intellectual challenges of the rest of the century. And it not only teaches our children facts but also teaches them how to think and learn on their own.

CULTURE

Teaching diversity prepares students to be global citizens by exposing them to people from different cultures and social groups.

DISCIPLINE

Students are the future assets of the nation. Discipline lays a good foundation of being selective, independent, punctual, focused, encouraged & organized in life.
