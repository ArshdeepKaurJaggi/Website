Our Building :

The school was established in 1984 by its founder and now director Mr Damodar Singh Jaggi, with the aim of providing education to all. As education is the building block of each students life.
From the past 36 years we are dedicated to provide this service and will continue to do so with utmost dedication and selflessness.
